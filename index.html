Nikola Tesla

(1856-1943)

Nikola Tesla can be considered as one of the most important investors of the 19th and early 20th century. His contribution to the invention and development of electromagnetism and electrodynamics are outstanding. Tesla contributed by various patents to other areas of engineering including telecommunications, remote control and mechanic engineering.
We can credit him for the idea of wireless internet and smartphones.

Believe it or not, in 1901, Tesla actually had the ideas of a wireless way to gather information, code it in his lab, chnage the frequency, and broadcast it to a hand-held device nearly exactly how our smartphones work today. Like many of Tesla's great ideas, it was never created or tested.

Tesla's inventions include:

  • AC Power (Alternating Current)
  • Tesla Coil
  • Magnifying Transmitter
  • Tesla Turbine
  • Shadowgraph
  • Radio
  • Neon Lamp
  • Hydroelectric Power
  • Induction Motor
  • Radio Controlled Boat
